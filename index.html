Erasable Tic Tac Toe

A classic game of X's and O's with a twist

How to Play

  • Players take turns placing X and O on the 3x3 grid
  • The first player to get 3 of their marks in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) wins
  • If all 9 squares are filled without a winner, the game is a tie
  • Time Challenge: Each player has 2 seconds to make their move. If you don't move in time, your last move will be erased! If you still don't move, your second-last move will be erased, and so on.
  • Click "New Game" to start a fresh round
  • Toggle between "vs AI" and "vs Player" to play single player or 2 players respectively
